Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Overview

Screenshot Translator ("the Extension") is developed by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

2. What the Extension Does

Screenshot Translator allows you to capture regions of your screen, extract text using optical character recognition (OCR), and translate that text between languages.

3. Data We Collect

Account Data: Email address, collected when you register.

Usage Data: Translation usage counts, used to manage subscription limits.

User Content: Words you save to your personal dictionary.

Local Data: Extension settings stored locally in Chrome, never transmitted to our servers.

We do not collect the content of screenshots or translated text beyond what is necessary to process your request in real time. That content is not stored or logged.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your account and manage your subscription.
  • To track usage against your plan limits.
  • To store your saved dictionary words.
  • To process payments for paid plans.

We do not sell your data to third parties under any circumstances.

6. Data Storage & Security

Account data is stored in Supabase on secure cloud infrastructure. Extension settings remain on your device. All transmissions use encrypted HTTPS connections.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained while your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed.

8. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Export your dictionary data upon request.

9. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will reflect changes.
