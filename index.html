How It Works

1. Press the shortcut

Hit Cmd+Shift+X (or click the extension icon) to activate the capture mode.

2. Select the area

Drag to select any region of your screen containing text.

3. Instant translation

Text is extracted and translated in seconds. A floating popup shows the result.

4. Save words

Click any word to add it to your personal dictionary for later review.
